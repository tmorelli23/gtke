Hey everyone, my name is Tony Morelli. I am originally from Tinley Park, Illinois and I am
the youngest of six kids. I am a senior here at Lewis University, majoring in Aviation
Administration. I joined the United States Air Force after I graduated high school and I
work as an aircraft mechanic on the KC-135 Stratotanker. The KC-135's primary mission

specialize in fuel system maintenance on this aircraft, and my duties consist of

opportunity to travel all over the world and I have been to several places and
countries that I never would have been able to travel to.
